Srila Gour Govinda Swami Maharaja

TRINAD API SUNICENA

"You should first examine your heart to check
that there are no holes in it. Is it a pure
heart? If there are holes or nasty things
in it, how can you get prema-rasa? Lust, anger, greed,
pride, arrogance, and envy are the nasty things. These
are the holes in the container. Though Mahaprabhu
gives krsna-prema indiscriminately to one and all,
why is it that one and all are not receiving it? When
will our hearts be free from all these nasty things?
How can we take care of the container, the heart,
that we may receive Mahaprabhu’s mercy and make a
success of our human birth?"
