tam adi-devam karuna-nidhanam
tamala-varnam suhitavataram
apara-samsara-samudra-setum
bhajamahe bhagavata-svarupam

We worship the original Lord, the ocean of mercy, whose bodily
complexion is just like a tamala tree. He appears in this world
for the welfare of all, he is the bridge for crossing the unfathomable
ocean of material existence, and his body is Srimad-Bhagavatam.
— Padma Purana
