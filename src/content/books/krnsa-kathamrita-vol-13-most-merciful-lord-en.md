tumi-o karuna-sindhu pataki janara bandhu
ebara karaha yadi tyaga
patita-pavana nama nirmala se anupama
tahate lagaye bada daga

You are an ocean of mercy. You are the friend of the sinners. If
you abandon me now, then your peerless and glorious reputation
as patita-pavana (purifier of the fallen) is only a great trick.
