Braja Bandhu Manik was born in a Vaisnava
family, surrounded by the chanting of the
holy name and educated in the Srimad Bhaga-
vad-gita, the Srimad-Bhagavatam, Sri Caitanya-caritamrta
and Sanskrit from very early in life. In his later years, he
severed all ties to his family, changed his name to Gour
Gopalananda dasa and travelled the length and breadth of
Bharata (India) in search of a self-realized soul (tattva-darsi
guru) who could give him sannyasa. His life’s aspiration
from childhood had been to take sannyasa and preach
the mission of Sriman Gauranga Mahaprabhu. His search
came to fruition in Vrndavana 1974 when he finally met his
spiritual master, His Divine Grace Srila A.C. Bhaktivedanta
Swami Prabhupada, who awarded him the sannyasa order
of life in 1975 in Vrndavana.
