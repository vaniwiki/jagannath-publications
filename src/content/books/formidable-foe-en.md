"There is a difference between kama and
prema — lust and love. Every jiva in this
material world has the desire to enjoy.
They are greatly selfish. They run after
sense enjoyment. But those who are
mahatmas, great personalities, maha-
bhagavatas, great devotees, they run
after giving pleasure and happiness to
the senses of Krishna."
