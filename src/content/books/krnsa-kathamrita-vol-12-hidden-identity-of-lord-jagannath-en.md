aparupa ratha
nace gaura-raya sabe meli gaya
yata yata maha-bhage
Lord Gaura Ray dances before the wonderful cart. Gathering around him,
the glorious and fortunate devotees sing.
bhavete avasa ki rati divasa
aveSe kichu na jane
jagannatha-mukha dekhi maha-sukha
nace gara gara mane
In his ecstasy, he does not know if it is day or night. Gazing happily at Lord
Jagannath’s face, Sri Gaurasundar dances, overwhelmed with ecstatic love.
