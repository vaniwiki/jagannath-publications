A portable Bluetooth speaker preloaded with the complete audio collection of Sri Srimad Gour Govinda Swami Maharaja.

**2,200+ audio files** containing over **3,000 hours** of lectures, bhajans, and conversations in high-quality 128 GB format.

- **Srimad-Bhagavatam lectures** — Complete series
- **Bhagavad-gita lectures** — Verse-by-verse commentary
- **Caitanya-caritamrta lectures** — In-depth discussions
- **Darsana lectures** — Personal meetings and exchanges
- **Festival lectures** — Special occasion addresses
- **Bhajans** — Devotional songs and kirtans

- Amkette Pocket Mate Bluetooth Speaker
- FM Radio built-in
- USB / SD card player
- Number pad for track selection
- Up to 12 hours battery life
- Type-C charging
- 5W powerful output
- Compact and portable
