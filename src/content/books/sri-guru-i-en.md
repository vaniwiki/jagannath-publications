Sri Guru Makes Krsna Appear in Your Heart

"Krsna's appearance in the heart
of a jiva is guru-krpa. One who has gurutva, who is heavy (guru means
heavy, not light, laghu); he is gurudeva. One who can make Krsna appear
in any place, in the heart of any jiva; he is guru. He has gurutva. If your
heart is filled with dirt, will Krsna appear there? Will Krsna appear in
such a filthy place? No, but a sadhu-guru can make Krsna appear in that
heart. One who can do that, he is guru. Can you get Krsna? Do you have
the qualification? Have you ever imagined that you will get Krsna? Can
you, a conditioned soul, with a filthy heart, a contaminated heart, make
Krsna appear? No! This is the activity of sadhu-gurus, the dear devotees,
the Vaisnavas. The conditioned, degraded soul has no qualification at
all to get Krsna. Does he have any right? No! He has no right at all. He
does not deserve it."
— Excerpt from Chapter 7
