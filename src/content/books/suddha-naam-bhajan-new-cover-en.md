Suddha-nama Bhajana

“If you are really chanting the pure name, it is non-
different from Krsna and it is non-different from
the form of Krsna. The name of Krsna, the form of Krsna
and Krsna Himself are non-different. Sri-nama smarile
rupa aise sange sange, if you are chanting the pure name or
remembering the pure name, you will immediately see the
all-beautiful form of Syamasundara. The different lilas will
manifest before you. That is the test that you are chanting
the pure name. You see Syamasundara immediately.”
— Excerpt from Chapter Two - Namabhasa
