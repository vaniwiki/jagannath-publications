The feet of Sri Guru are compared to a lotus,
but this lotus grows in the transcendental water of
bhakti-rasa. Such are the lotus feet
of Sri Guru. His lotus feet are aprakrta, not material but
transcendental. They are made of transcendental
bhakti-rasa — bhakti-sadma, and all madhuri, beauty.
