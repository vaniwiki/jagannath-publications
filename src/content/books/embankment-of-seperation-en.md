Compiled from a series of discourses by
Sri Srimad Gour Govinda Swami Maharaja

“The pastimes of Sri Caitanya Mahaprabhu are like an unlimited
ocean. That ocean swells up, and the topmost swelling is here in
Jagannatha Puri Dhama because Mahaprabhu manifested his
final pastimes there. When Mahaprabhu was dancing such a
wonderful dance before Lord Jagannatha the whole world became
amazed. Not only the whole world, but even Jagannatha became
amazed. This is because the root of relishing this mellow is
amazement.”
