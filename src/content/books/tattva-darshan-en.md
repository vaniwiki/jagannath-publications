This first volume in a new series compiles
questions and answers from after-class
discussions and darshan sessions with Sri Srimad
Gour Govinda Swami. A respected teacher in
the Gaudiya Vaishnava tradition, he provides
clear, scripturally grounded answers along with
practical guidance on devotional life.

Through these exchanges, he addresses
key aspects of spiritual practice and the
challenges that come with it. Drawing from
the Bhagavad-gita, Srimad-Bhagavatam, and
the teachings of Sri Caitanya Mahaprabhu and
previous Gaudiya Acharyas, this book serves as a
valuable resource for anyone seeking to deepen
their Krishna consciousness.
