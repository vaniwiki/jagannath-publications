"If you satisfy Lord Hari then ultimately you will develop
ruci, taste for hari-katha. Then you will have
come to the proper path, the pure path of devotional
service, mukhya-patha; that is the chief path. From gauna,
the secondary path, you will be elevated to the chief path,
from gauna to mukhya. It will help you if you are under the
expert guidance of a bona fide guru."
