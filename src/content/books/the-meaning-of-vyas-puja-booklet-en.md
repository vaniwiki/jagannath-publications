The Meaning of Vyasa-puja

“Srila Bhaktisiddhanta Sarasvati
Thakura Gosvami Prabhupada
Maharaja introduced the first
Vyasa-puja in our Gaudiya line on his
fiftieth anniversary, and this has been duly performed from that time.
Then on his fifty-second anniversary, Srila Bhaktisiddhanta
Sarasvati Prabhupada Maharaja gave a lecture on Srimad-
Bhagavatam and in conclusion he presented the meaning of
Vyasa-puja. He said, 'Vyasa-puja means the puja of all Gaudiya
Vaisnava acaryas.' Therefore from this statement we
understand the purport of Vyasa-puja. It is not only the puja
of Vyasadeva, or only the puja of Krsna, or only the puja of
Gaurasundara, or only the puja of guru. That is not real Vyasa-
puja. Vyasa-puja means the puja of all suddha, pure Gaudiya
Vaisnava acaryas. That is real Vyasa-puja.”
