The guru's passing away is only apparent. guru is always there. "I am eternally his disciple, and he is eternally my guru." His passing away is only apparent. He is always with us. We have such
a loving relationship. Although he is not present here, although he's gone back home, back to Godhead, from that Vrajabhumi, eternal Goloka Vrindavan, he has his eye on his dear disciple and takes care of him.

Sri Srimad Gour Govinda Swami Maharaja
