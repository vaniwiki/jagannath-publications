From Sri Srimad Gour Govinda Swami Maharaja

The pastimes of Sri Caitanya Mahaprabhu are like
an unlimited ocean. That ocean swells up, and the
topmost swelling is here in Jagannath Puri Dhama,
because Mahaprabhu manifested his final pastimes
there. When Mahaprabhu was dancing such a
wonderful dance before Lord Jagannatha, the
whole world became amazed. Not only the whole
world, but even Jagannath became amazed. This is
because the root of relishing this mellow is amazement!
