The ecstatic love for Krishna possessed by the
residents of Vrindavan far excels that found in any
other place. When Krishna suddenly departed for
Mathura he plunged the vrajavasis into an ocean of
sorrow. What was Krishna’s purpose in leaving?
What happened when his messenger Uddhava
arrived in Vraja with a message of love? And, most of
all, how is it that Krishna never leaves Vrindavan?
