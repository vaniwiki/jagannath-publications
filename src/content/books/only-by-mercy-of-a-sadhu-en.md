“Those who have smeared the ointment of love on their eyes,
premanjana-cchurita-bhakti-vilocanena, who are endowed
with bhakti-caksu, the vision of devotion, always see the
all-beautiful Syamasundara Krsna,
within and without. You have to associate with such
sadhu-guru-vaisnavas. They have bound up Syamasundara
Krsna with the rope of love in their hearts. If you surrender
unto such sadhu-guru, serve him, please him, and get his
mercy then you will be endowed with that vision and
can see the beautiful form of Lord Syamasundara, within
and without. This is the only means.”
