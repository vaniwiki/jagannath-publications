Maharaja’s consciousness was
deeper than I’d been used to.”

“I treasure every word of When Good Fortune Arises,
about the life and teachings of Sripada Gour Govinda Swami
Maharaja, for they bring us close to Srila Prabhupada.”

“When Good Fortune Arises is the perfect title for a book which
not only chronicles the life and teachings of Gour Govinda
Maharaja, but which also includes captivating memories of those
whose lives were impacted by their encounters with him.”
