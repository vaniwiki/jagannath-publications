When many different people come
together, then different opinions will
emerge. Fighting, quarrel and disagreement
will naturally arise. Satyam eva jayate —
truth will eventually be established, but it
takes time. It is not so easy. If there was no
darkness how could you glorify the light?
We have to tolerate. We have to have
patience and cooperate. That is the
instruction of sadhus and acaryas. This is
Mahaprabhu’s process. It is so sweet and
nice. Unless we learn Mahaprabhu’s
teachings, how can we get his mercy?
